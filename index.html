My James Bond Tribute Page

Growing up I was huge fan of James Bond. I bought Goldfinger when I was twelve years old at a Suncoast Video. I swear I watched that movie fifty times in the first month. From the opening with Shirly Bassey, to Oddjob with his razor hat. I fell in love with this character, and this world. To be honest this is my second Bonnd tribute page. The first opening I did for my senior project, which of course didn't work the day I presented, but I did pass.
